Continuous-Time Chaotic RNN

Numerical integration of: τ(dx/dt) = -x + g * J * tanh(x)
Observing the thermodynamic limit phase transition to chaos (Sompolinsky et al., 1988; Abbott, 2016).

Controls the variance of the recurrent weights. $g < 1$: stable fixed point. $g > 1$: chaotic regime.

Rebuilds structural matrix $O(N^2)$. Sharpness of phase transition depends on $N \to \infty$.

Adjusts the horizontal scale to visualize fast vs. slow timescales of the attractor.

Kicks the membrane potentials to observe decay or divergence.

Neurons (N): 250
Plotted Units: 10
Integration step (dt): 1.0 ms
