Continuous-Time Chaotic RNN

Numerical integration of: τ(dx/dt) = -x + g * J * tanh(x) + u * z
Observing thermodynamic chaos and FORCE Learning (Sussillo & Abbott, 2009).

Controls the variance of recurrent weights. Must be > 1.0 for robust learning.

FORCE Learning

Target: $f(t) = \sin(2\pi \cdot 2\text{Hz} \cdot t)$. When training is off, the network runs autonomously using the learned weights.
