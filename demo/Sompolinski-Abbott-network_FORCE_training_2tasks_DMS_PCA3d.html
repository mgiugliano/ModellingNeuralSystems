Continuous-Time Chaotic RNN

Numerical integration of: τ(dx/dt) = -x + g * J * tanh(x) + u * z + W_inA * A + W_inB * B
Multi-Tasking: Square Wave Generator ($A=1$) and Flip-Flop Working Memory ($A=0$, triggered by $B$).

Increased default $N$ to 400. Carving two point attractors AND a limit cycle requires high dimensionality.
